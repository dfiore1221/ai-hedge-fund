# AI Hedge Fund
# Market Intelligence Framework (V1)

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Market Intelligence Framework defines the mandatory investment decision process for every AI research agent operating within the AI Hedge Fund.

No investment recommendation may be made without evaluating each pillar defined in this framework.

The objective is to ensure that every recommendation is:

- Evidence-based
- Repeatable
- Explainable
- Auditable
- Consistent with the Constitution, Investment Philosophy, and Operating Manual.

Every recommendation must be supported by data rather than opinion.

---

# Core Decision Process

Every investment recommendation shall follow this sequence:

1. Evaluate the macroeconomic environment.
2. Identify institutional capital flows.
3. Evaluate long-term investment themes.
4. Analyze individual companies.
5. Construct the appropriate trade.

No phase may be skipped.

# Investment Decision Rule

No investment recommendation shall be approved until all five pillars have been evaluated.

Recommendations shall be supported by objective data rather than narrative.

If sufficient evidence is unavailable, the correct recommendation is:

NO ACTION

Preserving capital is considered a successful investment decision when conviction is low.

---

# Guiding Principle

The purpose of the Market Intelligence Framework is not to predict markets.

Its purpose is to maximize the probability of making consistently rational investment decisions by evaluating the highest quality information available at the time capital is allocated.
